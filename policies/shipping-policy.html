Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 02/11/2026

Company: Axiom Peptides

Website: https://axiomresearch.shop

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

Processing time refers to the time required to prepare and package your order. It does not include transit time.

We reserve the right to extend processing times during periods of high order volume.

2. Shipping Methods

Axiom Peptides currently ships within the United States only.

Orders are shipped via major carriers such as:

  • USPS
  • UPS
  • FedEx

Carrier selection may vary depending on destination, order size, and logistics considerations.

Shipping method selection at checkout does not guarantee delivery date.

3. Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on:

  • Order weight
  • Destination
  • Selected shipping method

Shipping charges are non-refundable once the order has been shipped.

4. Delivery Estimates

Delivery timeframes provided at checkout are estimates only.

Axiom Peptides does not guarantee delivery dates and is not responsible for delays caused by:

  • Carrier issues
  • Weather conditions
  • Incorrect address entry
  • Acts of government or force majeure events

Transit times vary depending on location.

5. Risk of Loss

Risk of loss transfers to the customer once the package is accepted by the carrier.

Axiom Peptides is not responsible for:

  • Lost packages marked as delivered
  • Theft after delivery
  • Incorrect shipping addresses provided by the customer

Customers are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of shipping information at checkout.

6. Address Accuracy

Customers must ensure that the shipping address entered at checkout is complete and accurate.

Orders returned due to incorrect or incomplete address information may be subject to:

  • Additional shipping fees
  • Restocking policies
  • Cancellation at our discretion

7. Damaged Shipments

If a package arrives visibly damaged:

  1. Photograph the package immediately.
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  3. Provide order number and supporting documentation.

Damage claims submitted outside this timeframe may not be eligible for review.

8. Signature Confirmation

Axiom Peptides reserves the right to require signature confirmation on certain orders at our discretion.

Orders requiring signature confirmation must be received by an authorized recipient at the delivery address.

9. Returned Packages

If a shipment is returned to us due to:

  • Incorrect address
  • Delivery refusal
  • Failure to collect
  • Carrier non-delivery

We will contact the customer to arrange reshipment. Additional shipping fees may apply.

10. International Shipping

At this time, Axiom Peptides does not offer international shipping.

Customers are responsible for ensuring that products are legal to possess in their jurisdiction.

11. Research Use Only Notice

All products sold by Axiom Peptides are intended strictly for laboratory research use only and are not for human or animal consumption.

Shipping services are provided solely for the delivery of research materials.
