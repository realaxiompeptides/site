How should peptides be stored?

For optimal stability, lyophilized peptides should be stored in a cool, dry environment below 32°F (0°C). Once reconstituted, peptides should be stored in a refrigerator between 36°F and 46°F. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles and prolonged exposure to light or heat, as this may degrade the compound and reduce stability.
